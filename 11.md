## Problem 13

The length of a whole poll: N(*d*poll+Q/R).

The number of bits transferred in a poll: NQ.

So the maximum throughput of the broadcast channel: 

NQ/N(*d*poll+Q/R) = Q/(*d*poll+Q/R).
